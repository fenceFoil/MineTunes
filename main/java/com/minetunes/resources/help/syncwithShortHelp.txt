SyncWith [voice] [optional layer]

Advances or retreats the voice to the current time of another voice.

Example:

V0 Aist Btqw
V1 I10
SyncWith 0
Cmajw

If [layer] isn't given, SyncWith syncs with the longest layer in [voice] automatically.

A more specific version of SyncVoices.
