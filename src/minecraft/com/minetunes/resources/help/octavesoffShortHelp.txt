OctavesOff [numbers]

Turns off all or individual octaves from the Octaves keyword.

[numbers]: Optional. If not given, all octaves are turned off.

Examples:

-------------
Octaves 1 2
A B C6
OctavesOff 2
A B C6
-------------

Plays three notes like this:

A5+A6+A7 B5+B6+B7 C6+C7+C8

Then plays them again without the topmost note:

A5+A6 B5+B6 C6+C7

See: Octaves
